# CRM & VOC Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project focuses on CRM analytics, sales pipeline monitoring, Voice of Customer (VOC) analysis, and campaign performance tracking using Power BI and Excel.

The dashboard was designed to help business teams monitor lead conversion, customer feedback trends, revenue performance, and marketing effectiveness through KPI-driven reporting and interactive visualizations.

---

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* Excel
* DAX
* Data Visualization
* KPI Reporting

---

## Dashboard Features

* Lead Acquisition Analysis
* CRM Funnel Tracking
* Conversion Rate Monitoring
* Revenue by Region
* VOC Feedback Insights
* Campaign Performance Analytics
* Interactive Slicers

---

## Key Business Insights

* Referral leads contribute high acquisition volume
* Revenue contribution varies significantly by region
* Customer complaints are concentrated around product quality and delivery delays
* Campaign conversion rates remain below 20%
